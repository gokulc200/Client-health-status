Vitamins & Minerals

Fat-soluble vitamins (vitamin A, D, E and K) are mainly found in: animal fats,vegetable oils, dairy foods, liver, fish

Water-soluble vitamins (vitamin C, the B vitamins and folic acid) are mainly found in: fruit and vegetables, grains, milk and dairy foods

Minerals include calcium and iron amongst many others and are found in: meat, cereals, fish, milk and dairy foods, fruit and vegetables, nuts.

Grains: Rolled or steel cut oats, Whole-wheat pasta, Whole-wheat tortillas, breads, and rolls, Brown or wild rice, Barley, whole corn, and cracked wheat
